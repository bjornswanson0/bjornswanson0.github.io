Six days a week, before the city wakes up. Training is the keel of my day — the discipline everything else gets built on. The goal has never been bulk; it's being lean, strong, and nimble for a long time.

Golf & the boys

An honest golf swing is a lifelong project, and I'm a willing student. Every summer the college crew reunites for our own major — four days, one trophy, and a group chat that never cools off.

Building things
